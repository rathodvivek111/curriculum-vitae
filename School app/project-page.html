Education management APp

Description

My main objective was to create an Android Application for the parents and teachers of the schools which provides all information about school activities like events, news, notices, gallery etc. Basically whole interface is divided into two parts one is for login and other is for userhome. Userhome divided into 5 parts which are news, events, notice, gallery and info. By implementing this project, I learnt
how basic management software can be developed in to android and php. It is used for the feedback to the parents , that how their children are doing in the school and which activities are going on in the school.
